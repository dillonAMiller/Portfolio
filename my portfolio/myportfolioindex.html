List Site
I created this website to input and track retail furniture display data. My reps can easily complete the checklist to let me know which displays and marketing materials need to be ordered. Additionally, the ability to maintain a real time database of this information saved time for the reps as well as greatly reduced the lead time for replacing worn retail displays.
